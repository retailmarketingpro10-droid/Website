Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products and services

What is your pricing model?

We offer transparent, one-time purchase pricing with optional annual renewal options. There are no hidden fees, processing charges, or surprise costs. Contact us for detailed pricing based on your business requirements.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer demo access to evaluate our products before purchasing. This gives you hands-on experience with the system and helps you determine if it's the right fit for your business.

Do you charge per transaction or per user?

No, we don't charge per transaction or per user. Our model is based on a one-time or annual subscription. This means you can have unlimited transactions and users without worrying about variable costs.

Do your systems work offline?

Yes! All our systems are built with offline-first architecture. You can continue working even if the internet goes down. Data automatically syncs when connectivity is restored, so you never lose information.

What hardware do I need for RMP POS?

RMP POS works on our Win B88 terminals, Android devices (N10/N50/N80 series), or compatible tablets/computers. We can help you choose the right hardware based on your business type and volume.

Is internet connectivity mandatory?

No. You can operate completely offline. Internet is only needed for syncing data to the cloud and accessing multi-location dashboards. This makes our system perfect for areas with unreliable connectivity.

How long does setup take?

Basic setup typically takes 1-3 days depending on your business complexity. We provide onboarding support and training to ensure your team is comfortable with the system. Migration from legacy systems may take additional time.

Can I migrate data from my existing system?

Absolutely! Our Inventory Migrator is designed to handle data from Tally, Excel, and other legacy systems. We ensure all your historical data is preserved and properly formatted for seamless transition.

Do you provide integration with payment gateways?

Yes, we integrate with popular payment gateways for digital payments. We also support UPI, debit cards, credit cards, and our system can work with offline cash-only businesses.

What kind of customer support do you offer?

We provide dedicated customer support via phone, email, and support ticket system. Our team helps with technical issues, training, and business optimization. Premium plans include priority support.

Do you provide training for my staff?

Yes! We provide comprehensive training for your entire team. This includes system navigation, daily operations, troubleshooting, and reporting. Training can be remote or on-site based on your needs.

How often do you update the software?

We release updates regularly with new features and improvements. All updates are backward compatible, and you'll receive notifications about new features. Updates are free for all users.

Is RMP POS suitable for small businesses?

Yes! RMP POS is designed for all business sizes. Our simple, intuitive interface works great for single-store operations, and our multi-location dashboard scales up to large chains.

Can I use it for multiple types of businesses (retail, restaurant, medical)?

Absolutely! RMP POS is industry-agnostic and works for retail stores, restaurants, medical clinics, refilling centers, and service businesses. We customize reporting and features based on your industry.

Is Carat Control only for jewelry stores?

While Carat Control is optimized for jewelry and workshops, its core inventory and staff management features work for any manufacturing or craftsmen-focused business.
