Products (Selected Overview)

A short summary of flagship apps and select features — focused, offline-first, and owner-forward.

RMP 1.0 POS

Mobile-first POS for single-location and chain retailers. Offline-capable with optional periodic sync.

  • No processing fees for paid installs (owner-controlled pricing).
  • Receipts by email/SMS; invoice includes UPI barcode for easy payments.
  • Reports (PDF downloads), transaction monitoring, and role-based access (Owners/Managers).
  • Offline transactions stored locally and synced automatically or on-demand.

Craftsmen & Inventory Manager

Inventory and workforce tracking for craftsmen, services, and retail — offline-ready and multi-company.

  • Products, stock, suppliers, and craftsmen tracking; GST calculator with HSN support.
  • Low-stock alerts, CSV bulk import/export, and multi-location dashboard.
  • Demo: Glide demo

Tally Migrator / Inventory Manager

Tally-style migration assistant for accountants and field executives — tailored exports and offline sync.

  • Multi-company switching, GST-aware exports, and Tally-friendly formats.
  • Designed to assist migration from legacy systems with offline operations.

CRM & Merchant Admin

Central admin to track signups, transaction volumes (RMP POS), support tickets, and onboarding tasks.

  • Merchant metrics: total merchants, loyal merchants, and processing volume.
  • Manual merchant add/edit, billing visibility, and support ticketing.
